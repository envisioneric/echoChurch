About Us

Our Story

  • 2008

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Pastor Andy’s family, along with three other families, moved together from across the country to start South Bay Church. They were propelled by a vision to start a church that communicated the timeless truths of Scripture in a way that could be practically applied and understood, even for those who felt irreligious and far from God. From the beginning, South Bay Church has envisioned becoming a church that would impact Silicon Valley with such a love and kindness that our city would be sad to see us leave.

  • February 2009

    A Church is Launched

    We began meeting at Don Callejon Elementary School in Santa Clara. After three years, we outgrew the school and moved into the building that is now the North San Jose Campus.

  • February 2014-2016

    Spreading God's Work

    South Bay Church had continued to grow and we launched a second campus in Sunnyvale. God’s miracles continued, allowing us to plant our third campus in South San Jose.

  • January 28th, 2018

    A Big Change

    As we continued expanding, we sensed God leading our church to share God’s love beyond the South Bay area. We officially changed our name to Echo.church.
